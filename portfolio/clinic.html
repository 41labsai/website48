About Us

Dr. Rachel Tan & Team

Dr. Rachel Tan graduated from NUS Medicine in 2009 and has been serving the Orchard community for over 15 years. She believes in building long-term relationships with her patients — treating the person, not just the symptoms.

Our clinic is equipped with modern diagnostic tools and staffed by a friendly, multilingual team (English, Mandarin, Malay). We accept CHAS, MediSave, and all major insurance panels.
