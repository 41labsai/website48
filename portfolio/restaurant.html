Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 2008

The Golden Fork started as Chef Marco Russo's dream — a place where authentic Italian flavors meet modern techniques. Every dish tells a story, from the hand-rolled pasta to the wood-fired pizzas.

We source our ingredients from local farms and import specialty items directly from Italy. No shortcuts, no compromises — just honest food made with love.
